## Functional Requirements

### Map & Path-finding
- Show a map of the stadium with POIs
- Show heat-map that displays the amount of people
- Path-finding to services and places (bars, entrance, seat, etc.)
- Options for path-finding (avoiding stairs, etc.)

### Wait Times & Dynamic Re-routing
- Show estimated wait times for queues in services
- List of services sorted by wait time and distance
- Dynamic re-routing based on wait times

### Emergency
- In case of a big emergency, show an evacuation route

### Settings (Usability)
- Must support different languages and allow easy switching
- Notification customisation (emergency alerts)

### Ticket Handling
- Must scan the user ticket
- Show seat POI based on ticket(s) information
